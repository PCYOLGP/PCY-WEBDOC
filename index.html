Welcome to the Our Lady of Guadalupe Parish, Parish Commission on Youth! We're a group of young people from our parish who get together to have fun, learn, and help out in our community. Whether you're interested in volunteering, getting to know more about your faith, or just hanging out with friends, there's something here for everyone.

GSFF 2022

GSFF is a short film festival of Our Lady of Guadalupe Parish in Marilao, Bulacan. First, it is exclusive only to the Parish Commission on Youth Organization, sooner or later it will be open to other organizations and parishes.
